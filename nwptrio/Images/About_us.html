Welcome Walka-family!

We are a group of students from San Jose State University. This is our first group and semester working with each other. Our group name is called NWP, which represents the first letter of our own first names. N stands for Nicole, W stands for Wanjing, and P stands for Pyae Phyo Aung. With Walka, we hope to deliver simple yet eye-catching shirt designs to our fans. Aside from our t-shirt business, we hope to give back to our community as well. By purchasing Walkanation t-shirt, we will be contributing 100% of the proceeds in Walka-nation to any local San Jose public schools. Thank you for visiting our site and we hope to see you again!
